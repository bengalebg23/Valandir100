
## Scene Setup

As the party boards Cornelius's ship at the south docks, Helios sends a squad to stop them leaving. The fight is a **split-focus encounter** — some party members battle enforcers on the dock while others frantically free the mooring ropes. Once all four ropes are cut, the ship moves regardless of who's still on board. Anyone not on deck has **one round to jump or gets left behind.**

### Traits

**Gang Tactics.** The enforcer has advantage on attack rolls against a creature if at least one of the enforcer's allies is adjacent to the target and not incapacitated.

**Dockside Fighter.** The enforcer ignores difficult terrain caused by ropes, rigging, crates, and similar dockside obstacles.

---

### Actions

**Multiattack.** The enforcer makes two melee attacks.

**Heavy Cudgel.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 7 (1d8+3) bludgeoning damage. On a hit, the target must succeed on a **DC 13 Strength saving throw** or be pushed 5 feet.

**Boarding Hook** *(Recharge 5–6).* *Ranged Weapon Attack:* +3 to hit, range 20 ft., one target. *Hit:* 5 (1d6+1) piercing damage and the target is **grappled** (escape DC 13). While grappled this way, the target cannot move more than 20 feet from the enforcer.

---

### Reactions

**Hold the Line.** When an ally within 5 feet is hit by a melee attack, the enforcer can impose disadvantage on that attack roll (declared before the roll is resolved).

## Encounter Design

### Enemy Waves

| Wave | Composition | Trigger |
|------|-------------|---------|
| Wave 1 | 4 Enforcers on the dock, blocking the gangplank | Start of encounter |
| Wave 2 | 3 Enforcers + 1 Lieutenant, arriving by boat from the harbour | Mid-fight escalation |

### Mooring Ropes

There are **4 mooring ropes**. The ship cannot leave until all 4 are freed.

Each rope requires one of the following:
- **DC 12 Athletics check** to untie, or
- **One attack roll** (AC 11, 5 HP, immune to poison and psychic damage) to cut

Enforcers will attempt to **re-secure freed ropes** using their Boarding Hook action if they are within range.

### Winning Condition

Once all 4 ropes are free, Cornelius calls it — **the ship moves**. Any party member not on board has **one round to jump** or is left behind in Valandir with Helios's men.
