# Vaelis at the Gala
*Velmoran Assassin — In Disguise*

## Cover Identity Tonight
**"Aldren Vayne"** — Velmoran trade attaché. Here to observe trade discussions and represent Velmoran commercial interests. Polite, forgettable, entirely plausible.

## Appearance Tonight
Different hair — shorter, swept back, no longer the style witnesses described. Charcoal grey formal coat, no [[Amberleaf Purfume]] — deliberately. He knows the party has been tracking the scent. A glass of wine he has not touched. Back to the wall at all times. Exit always in peripheral vision.

## What He Is Actually Doing
Watching [[Cornelius Thatch]]. Watching the party. Counting pieces on the board. He is not here to act tonight — the gala is too public, the consequences too visible. He is here to assess whether the party is going to be a problem on the expedition.

His conclusion, by the end of the evening: yes. Possibly a significant one. He will send word to Velmoran before the ship departs.

## The Amberleaf Decoy
He gave [[Lord Pell Dawnwick]] a complimentary bottle of Amberleaf cologne this morning — anonymously, delivered as a gift to his townhouse. Pell has been wearing it proudly all evening. Any player tracking the scent will find Pell instead of Vaelis. This was deliberate.

## Detection
- **Passive Perception 16+** — a player notices a man near the back wall who hasn't touched his drink and keeps his back to the wall
- **Insight DC 18** — something about his stillness is professionally wrong for a celebration
- **Perception DC 20** — despite different hair and no perfume, a player who met him at Highspire Manor recognises his posture and movement

## If Spotted
He holds eye contact for exactly one second too long. Then looks away first. Unhurried. Like he has already decided the observer is not a problem yet. He will not engage unless cornered. If approached he is perfectly pleasant, perfectly vague, and finds a reason to excuse himself within two minutes.

He does not run. Running draws attention.

## Key Lines
> *"Aldren Vayne. Velmoran trade office. Magnificent evening — the Library does these things well."*

> *(if pressed)* *"I'm afraid commercial discussions aren't terribly exciting dinner conversation. Tell me about yourselves."*

## What He Knows
- The party investigated Highspire Manor and found his room
- They presented a fake head to Helios — he finds this professionally impressive
- Cornelius is sending them on the expedition — this is a problem
- The Hearthstone's true significance — he works for people who do not want it found
