# Session 13 — The Gala
## Summary

### Pre-Gala — The Streets
The party dressed at the townhouse and made their way toward the gala. En route they found **Garrus Vanton** in a street brawl with Helios's men. The party intervened, convinced Helios's men to stand down, and pulled Garrus aside. They warned him to leave the city. Whether he actually did is unconfirmed.

---

### Arrival
**Mimbi** rode on the roof of the carriage playing music as they pulled up to the gala. A natural 20 on Performance. The entrance turned heads before they even walked through the doors.

---

### Hugo and Hector
Inside, **ThicketWhistle** cast Minor Illusion to make Hugo stumble as he approached his father. Classic TW chaos.

Hugo didn't let it shake him. He confronted **Hector de Léon** directly — accusing him of hiding behind pens and treaties rather than taking real action. Hector was cold and precise in response, noting that **Cornelius Thatch** was a man worth speaking to, and that Hugo's conduct reflected on House de Léon. The conversation ended without resolution — but something was different in the air.

---

### Hugo's Speech
Carried by the warmth Mimbi's performance had built in the room, Hugo took it further. He addressed the assembled nobility directly — a Persuasion roll of 19 — and publicly rallied them against Helios and the Broken Crown Syndicate. It was the first time anyone had named Helios openly in a room full of Valandir's elite.

It landed. A senior noble spoke up in agreement. Five or six families visibly aligned with Hugo's position before the evening was out.

---

### Cornelius Makes His Move
Off the back of Hugo's speech, **Cornelius Thatch** moved through the crowd and introduced himself. Warm, precise, faintly mischievous. He referenced Hector, noted that the party had just made themselves very prominent in a city that was becoming dangerous for them, and suggested somewhere quieter to talk.

He laid out the expedition pitch — an unmapped jungle island, the Verdant Sigil, the Vault of Khar'zeth, 10% of everything recovered, departure in four days.

**The party did not trust him. The expedition was declined.** They are staying in Valandir to fight.

---

### Vaelis — Identified, Tackled, Broken
**Vaelis Duskryn** was in the room in disguise — posing as a Velmoran trade attaché. Different hair, different clothes, no Amberleaf perfume. Watching Cornelius. Watching the party.

When he was made, he ran.

**Mog** tackled him — Athletics 23. Vaelis hit the floor, wounded and pinned in the middle of the gala. The crowd backed away. Glasses were dropped.

The party interrogated him. He resisted. They pushed. Eventually he cracked.

**Vaelis's confession:**
- He poisoned Marcell Veyron on orders from the Velmoran syndicate
- Marcell was assembling a strong rival team for Cornelius's expedition
- The Velmoran syndicate has their own team entered and wanted no competition
- Vaelis was sent to eliminate all viable rival teams before departure

Vaelis was handed to **Library guards** and taken into **Kermin Traylow's** custody. He is now a diplomatic flashpoint — the Library holds a Velmoran syndicate assassin.

---

### Hector's Moment
Before the carriages left, Hector found Hugo. He pulled him slightly aside. Quiet, measured — but something different in his eyes.

*"You made a mess of this evening."*

He straightened Hugo's collar. The first time he'd touched his son without it feeling like a correction.

*"But you made it count."*

He walked away. For Hector de Léon, that was a standing ovation.

---

### State of Play Leaving the Gala
- Vaelis in Library custody. Confession given.
- Five to six noble families aligned against Helios — fragile but real
- Cornelius's expedition declined. Party staying to fight.
- Bwark's note from Serena Silmore — unresolved. Bwark's player was absent.
- The party step outside into the night, still in their gala clothes, no armour, no weapons

They do not yet know what is waiting for them.
