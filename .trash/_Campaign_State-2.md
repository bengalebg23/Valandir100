## Active Threads (Priority Order)

### 🔴 1. Helios & the BCS — Critical / War Footing
- **Helios** is the new leader of the Broken Crown Syndicate (BCS). Ruthless, calculating, expanding aggressively.
- The party framed **Garrus Vanton** for a warehouse break-in — lie exposed when Garrus was seen alive by Helios's men.
- At the Gala, Hugo publicly rallied 5-6 noble families against Helios and the BCS. This is a direct and very public challenge to Helios's power in Valandir.
- Helios will know about this by morning. A severe response is likely within 24-48 hours.
- The party have chosen to **stay in Valandir and fight** rather than take Cornelius's expedition.
- **Status:** City-level political conflict now in motion. Hugo has noble allies but they are soft — not fighters. Helios has muscle. The party need a plan before Helios moves.

### 🔴 2. Bwark's Exposure — Unresolved
- Bwark treated Marcell Veyron at the Hellbrand Casino on the night Marcell died in the street.
- **Serena Silmore** (casino owner, in Helios's pocket) has identified Bwark as the healer.
- Note received at the Gala: *"The healer who treated Marcell Veyron is known to us. Come to the east corridor side entrance before the speeches end. Come alone. — A friend of Serena Silmore."*
- **Bwark's player was absent. The note meeting never happened. Carry forward.**
- **Status:** Still a ticking clock. Helios likely already knows via Serena regardless.

### 🟠 3. Vaelis Duskryn — In Library Custody
- Tall elf assassin from **Velmoran**. Killed Marcell Veyron using an exotic magical poison.
- At the Gala: attempted to flee, tackled by Mog (Athletics 23), wounded and pinned, interrogated until he cracked.
- **Confession:** Poisoned Marcell Veyron on orders from the Velmoran syndicate. Marcell was assembling a strong rival team for Cornelius's expedition. The Velmoran syndicate has their own team entered and wanted no competition. Vaelis was sent to eliminate all viable rival teams.
- **Handed to Library guards at the Gala.** Now in Kermin Traylow's custody.
- This is a significant diplomatic incident — the Library holds a Velmoran syndicate assassin.
- **Darric the bouncer** (Coaching Inn, south gate) was paid off by Vaelis the night of the murder. He is still uncontacted and has corroborating intel. Now potentially useful to the Library's case.
- **Status:** Captured. Confession given. Library has him. Velmoran syndicate will want him back or silenced.

### 🟠 4. Cornelius Thatch — Pitch Rejected
- Velmoran merchant and expedition sponsor. Approached Hugo at the Gala following Mimbi's Nat 20 performance and Hugo's rally speech.
- Pitch made: jungle island, Verdant Sigil, Vault of Khar'zeth, 10% contract, departs in four days.
- **The party do not trust Cornelius and have declined the expedition.** They are staying to fight Helios.
- Cornelius knows (or claims not to know) about the Velmoran syndicate team. Vaelis's confession complicates his position — the Library now has evidence the syndicate was interfering with his expedition.
- **Status:** Expedition rejected. Cornelius departs in four days regardless. May resurface.

### 🟡 5. Garrus Vanton — Warned Off
- The party intercepted Garrus before the Gala — he was in a fight with Helios's men. Party convinced Helios's men to leave him alone, took Garrus aside, and told him to leave the city.
- Whether he actually left is unconfirmed.
- **Status:** Warned off. Unconfirmed gone. Still a liability if he resurfaces.

### 🟡 6. Darric the Bouncer — Loose End
- Hellbrand Casino bouncer. Was paid off by Vaelis to give Marcell privacy on the night of the murder.
- Spooked after Marcell's death. Fled to the old Coaching Inn by the south gate.
- **Status:** Still at the Coaching Inn. Has not been found. Now relevant as corroboration for the Library's case against Vaelis.

### 🟡 7. Noble Alliance — New / Fragile
- Hugo's Persuasion 19 speech at the Gala, boosted by Mimbi's Nat 20 performance, rallied 5-6 noble families to oppose Helios and the BCS.
- These families are sympathetic but soft — political capital, not muscle.
- **Status:** Alliance formed. Needs to be activated before Helios moves. Hugo needs to turn this into something with teeth.

### 🟢 8. The Feywild — Long Arc
- Mimbi is originally from the Feywild. The suppression of magic surges on the material plane is causing "bleaching" of Feywild inhabitants.
- The party briefly visited the Feywild (Session 9) but did not resolve anything there.
- **Status:** Background. Will resurface.

### 🟢 9. The Library — Ongoing Relationship
- The party are on good terms with **Kermin Traylow** (head of the Library, heavyset Dragonborn).
- The Library now holds Vaelis — a significant asset and diplomatic flashpoint.
- The Library has a golden idol missing from a jungle island — may intersect with the Cornelius expedition.
- Books are going missing from the Library — magically tied volumes that should be impossible to remove permanently.
- **Status:** Relationship strengthened. Vaelis in custody is a shared interest.
