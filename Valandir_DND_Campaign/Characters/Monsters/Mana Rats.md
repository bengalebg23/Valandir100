
# 🐀 **Mana Rats (Standard Swarm Fodder)**

_Small beast (surge-infused), chaotic neutral_

These sewer rats have absorbed unstable ambient magic. Their fur flickers like heat shimmer, their eyes glow faint violet, and sparks jump between their teeth when they hiss.

---

## **Mana Rat**

**AC:** 12  
**HP:** 7 (2d6)  
**Speed:** 30 ft., climb 20 ft.

**STR** 6 | **DEX** 14 | **CON** 10 | **INT** 2 | **WIS** 10 | **CHA** 4

**Resistances:** lightning  
**Vulnerability:** thunder (shatters their unstable aura)

### **Abilities**
**⚡ Mana Bite — Melee Weapon Attack**  
_+4 to hit_, reach 5 ft., one target.  
Hit: **3 (1d4+1) piercing** + **1 lightning damage**.

**✨ Arc Flash (Recharge 5–6)**  
The rat releases a burst of unstable mana.  
All creatures within 5 ft make a **DC 11 Dex save** or take **1d6 lightning damage**.  
Other mana rats in the blast **also take damage** → chain reactions are possible.

**💫 Flicker Step (Reaction)**  
When a creature misses it with an attack, the rat blinks 5 ft. in a random direction (roll d8). It cannot pass through walls.
