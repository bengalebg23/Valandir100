🐀🐀 **Mana Rat Swarm (Stronger encounter)**

A skittering carpet of dozens of unstable, twitching rats. When they all shiver at once, the tunnel lights flicker.

**AC:** 13  
**HP:** 36 (8d8)  
**Speed:** 30 ft., climb 20 ft.

**Damage Resistances:** lightning, piercing, slashing  
**Damage Vulnerabilities:** thunder  
**Condition Immunities:** charmed, frightened, grappled, prone, restrained  
**Senses:** darkvision 60 ft.

### **Swarm Bite**
_+4 to hit_, 2d6 piercing + 1d6 lightning  
(half damage if the swarm is at half HP or less)

### **Arc Cascade (Recharge 5–6)**
The swarm erupts into a chaotic storm of sparks.  
Creatures within 10 ft must make a **DC 12 Dex save** or take **2d6 lightning**.  
If a Mana Rat (single creature) is within 20 ft, they **detonate**, taking max damage and adding **+1d6 lightning** to the burst.

### **Ambient Surge Instability**
Any spell cast within 10 ft of the swarm triggers a wild magic pulse.  
Roll 1d6:  
1 → caster takes 1d6 psychic  
2 → the swarm heals 1d6  
3 → caster teleports 5 ft  
4 → next attack has disadvantage  
5 → random spark damages a creature (1d6 lightning)  
6 → nothing happens
