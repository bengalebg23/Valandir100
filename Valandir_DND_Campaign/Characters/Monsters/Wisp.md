[[GREYWILLOW]]

Void-Touched Wisp
Tiny Aberration, Unaligned
CR 3 (use 1)
This is what happens when magic is pulled too fast — a tear that never finished forming.
AC 15
HP 38 (7d4+21)
Speed 0 ft, fly 40 ft (hover)
STR 4 (–3)
DEX 18 (+4)
CON 16 (+3)
INT 8 (–1)
WIS 12 (+1)
CHA 10 (+0)
Damage Resistances all nonmagical
Damage Immunities force
Condition Immunities grappled, prone, restrained
Senses darkvision 60 ft
Traits
Magic Vacuum
At the start of each creature’s turn within 10 ft, their movement speed is reduced by 10 ft until end of turn.
Spell Drain (Reaction)
When a spell is cast within 30 ft, the Wisp regains HP equal to the spell’s level × 5.
Actions
Void Pulse. Ranged Spell Attack: +6 to hit, range 30 ft
Hit: 10 (2d6+3) force damage
Collapse (Once per Combat).
The Wisp implodes. All creatures within 15 ft take 3d8 force damage (Dex save DC 14 for half).
The Wisp is destroyed.
