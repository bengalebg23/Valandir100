**Armor Class:** 13  
**Hit Points:** 22 (4d8 + 4)  
**Speed:** 40 ft.

### STR 14 (+2) | DEX 13 (+1) | CON 12 (+1) INT 4 (–3) | WIS 12 (+1) | CHA 6 (–2)

**Skills:** Perception +3  
**Senses:** darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 13  
**Proficiency Bonus:** +2

---
### **Ambient Instability**
When the Mana Hyena takes damage, roll a d6:
- **1–2:** A harmless spark cracks off its fur.
- **3–4:** It gains **+2 damage** on its next attack.
- **5–6:** It releases a **mana pulse** (see below).
When triggered, all creatures within 10 ft. must succeed on a **DC 11 Con save** or take **3 (1d6) force damage**.
### **Bite**
_Melee Weapon Attack:_ +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target  
**Hit:** 7 (1d8 + 2) piercing damage + **2 (1d4) force damage** from unstable mana.
### **Crackle Leap (Recharge 5–6)**
The hyena jumps up to 20 ft. and makes a bite attack.  
On a hit, the target must make a **DC 11 Dex save** or take **3 (1d6) lightning damage** from the hyena’s crackling paws.
