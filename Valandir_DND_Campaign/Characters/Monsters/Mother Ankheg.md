# **Brood-Mother Ankheg (CR 3)**

_A mid-boss the players can fear but still beat._

**Large Monstrosity (Unaligned)**  
**Armor Class:** 15 (heavy chitin)  
**Hit Points:** 60 (8d10 + 16)  
**Speed:** 30 ft., burrow 20 ft.

### STR 17 (+3) | DEX 11 (+0) | CON 15 (+2)
### INT 2 (–4) | WIS 12 (+1) | CHA 5 (–3)

**Senses:** Tremorsense 60 ft., darkvision 60 ft.  
Proficiency Bonus:** +2

---
## TRAITS

### **Brood Commander**
All Broodlings within 30 ft. have **advantage on their first attack each round**.
### **Tunneler**
Burrows through earth and stone, leaving stable tunnels.

---
## ACTIONS

### **Multiattack**
The Brood-Mother makes **two Bite attacks**.
### **Bite**
+5 to hit • reach 10 ft. • one target  
**Hit:** (1d8 + 3) piercing damage.
### **Acid Spray (Recharge 5–6)**
30-foot line, 5 feet wide.  
DC 13 Dex save.  
**Damage:** 3d8 acid, half on success.
