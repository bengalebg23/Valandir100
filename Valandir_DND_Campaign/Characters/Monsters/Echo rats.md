🐀🌫️ Echo Rat

Small aberration (surge-touched), unpredictable & skittery

These rats exist slightly out of sync with themselves. They flicker between positions, leaving afterimages that lag behind their real movements. Their squeaks echo a half-second late, unsettlingly distorted.


---

Echo Rat

AC: 13
HP: 9 (2d6+2)
Speed: 30 ft., climb 20 ft.

STR 5 | DEX 15 | CON 12 | INT 2 | WIS 12 | CHA 4

Resistances: psychic
Vulnerabilities: radiant
Condition Immunities: frightened
Senses: darkvision 60 ft.


---

🗡️ Echo Bite — Melee Attack

+5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature.
Hit: 1d4+2 piercing + 1d4 psychic.
On hit, target must succeed a DC 11 Wis save or gain Disadvantage on their next attack (their vision splits on impact).


---

🌫️ Flickering Duplicate (3/day)

The rat creates a semi-transparent echo of itself in an adjacent space (5 ft).
Until the start of its next turn:

attacks have disadvantage against the Echo Rat

creatures cannot make opportunity attacks against it

if the rat moves, the echo lingers and explodes with psychic static
→ Creatures within 5 ft of the echo take 1d4 psychic damage.



---

🎵 Reverberating Squeal (Recharge 5–6)

The rat emits a warped, delayed screech.
Creatures within 10 ft must make a DC 12 Wis save or become disoriented:

cannot take reactions
speed is halved
ranged attacks have disadvantage

Duration: 1 round
(Success → no effect)


---

🌀 Phase Skitter (Reaction)

When targeted by a melee attack it can see, the Echo Rat shifts between positions.
The attack roll must be rerolled, and the new result stands.
