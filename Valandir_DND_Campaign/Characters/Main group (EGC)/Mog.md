[[Duck’s Beak Amulet]]

Mog previously belonged to the warband of the bloody maw, but was kicked out when he became a warlock, as they hate all magic users. 

Ok, Mog was a simple warrior, considered a bit of an imbicile by the rest of the barbarian tribe, his intellectual capacity ranks somewhere between a baked potato and and a particularly thick pudding. so definitely not much of a go getter, and of no great rank, until during one battle he was hit on the head. When he came round, Duck was stood on his chest, staring into his eyes. It was a profound moment for Mog (or a moment of concussion, who knows) but in that moment Mog realised he had been chosen by Duck for greater things, and had been granted access to great powers. So Mog swore to follow duck from then on, and carry out his will. His tribe honoured him as a mighty warlock, until one day Duck told him it was time to leave (or duck wandered off, again, a baked potato). 

He wields his mighty staff of hitting (a maul with a particularly shiny rock on the end) and can fire magical shots from his powerfully magic sling (small shiny rocks)
