[[Gramdrom Halfbuster]]

Wondrous Item, rare (requires attunement by a druid or cleric)

A polished amber amulet set in hammered iron, shaped like a stylized flame embracing a droplet of water. When worn, the amulet seems to pulse softly in rhythm with the wearer’s heartbeat, as if mirroring their compassion and resolve.

**Life’s Embrace:**
Once per long rest, when you cast a healing spell, you can choose to grant an additional 1d8 hit points to one creature within range. 

**Beacon of Protection:**
While attuned, as a bonus action you can touch the amulet and grant yourself or an ally within 10 feet a shield of divine energy, giving +2 AC until the start of your next turn. 

This can be used a number of times equal to your Wisdom modifier (minimum 1) per long rest.

**Echo of Loss**
Once. When a creature you care about drops to 0 HP, you may choose to cast Revivify on them as an action, without expending a spell slot or material components. The target must be within 10 minutes of death. Using this feature causes the amulet to shatter and turn to ash
