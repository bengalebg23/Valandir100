[[Mog]]

Duck’s Beak Amulet
(Wondrous Item, Rare, Requires Attunement by Mog)

A curved golden beak hangs from a crude leather cord, polished to a shine that rivals the stones on Mog’s sling. 

**Mog’s Ritual**
Mog must spend 10 minutes talking to Duck or the amulet and performing odd rituals (feeding it crumbs, bowing to it, quacking back, etc.). 

In doing so, The amulet subtly shifts or points in the direction of whatever Duck wants Mog to notice.

**Protector’s Fury**
When Duck is within 30 ft and takes damage, Mog can immediately use his bonus action to make one extra melee attack with his maul or one extra ranged attack with his sling. This attack deals normal damage +1d6 force.

This can be used once per long rest

**Devoted Aura**
Other creatures can sense Mog’s complete devotion to Duck within 10 ft. This can lead to friendship, fear, confusion, or mockery, depending on the NPCs around him.
Once per long rest Mog has advantage on persuasion, intimidation if to do with duck

**Extra flavour**
Those around Mog for the first time may occasionally hear a “quack” in a soft, approving tone in their minds.
