[[Hilda Stronghold]]

Stonebraid’s Thunderaxe

Weapon (battleaxe), rare, requires attunement by a warrior with martial training

This heavy battleaxe has a braided copper-and-steel haft, echoing the fiery braids of its wielder. When swung, it emits a faint rumble like distant war drums.

**Thunderous Charge**
Once per short rest, when Hilda moves at least 20 feet straight toward an enemy and hits with an attack, the target must make a Strength saving throw (DC = 8 + Hilda’s proficiency bonus + Strength modifier) or be knocked prone and take extra thunder damage equal to Hilda’s level.

On a critical hit, the axe emits a burst of thunder, and all enemies within 10 feet of the target must make a Constitution saving throw or be pushed 10 feet away and take half the thunder damage.

**Soldier’s Resolve
While attuned, Hilda gains advantage on saving throws against fear effects and once per long rest can add a +2 bonus to an attack roll when charging into combat.

Roleplay Hook
The axe “hums” when there’s potential for glory nearby. If Hilda hesitates or backs down from a fight she should engage, the axe grows cold in her hands, as if disappointed by the absence of valor
