[[Danerius]]

Codex of the Scholar

Wondrous item (holy symbol), uncommon
Requires attunement by a cleric

This polished disk of silver and stone serves as both holy symbol and magical focus. Fine etched lines spiral across its surface, each marking a place Danerius has studied—libraries, temples, ruins—some known, some forgotten.

When held, faint script briefly surfaces, then fades, as if responding to deliberate thought.

**Scholarly Preparation**
When Danerius finishes a long rest, he may store a spell of 2nd level or lower that he does not currently have prepared into the disk

This represents not raw inspiration, but practiced recall — the right lesson, at the right time.

**Measured Intervention**
(Reaction, 1/Short Rest)
When a creature Danerius can see within 30 feet makes an attack roll or ability check, Danerius may use his reaction to subtly guide events.
He may add or subtract 1d4 from the roll after the roll is made but before the result is declared.

This guidance manifests as a quiet word, a recalled maxim, or a subtle gesture
