[[Stonebraid’s Thunderaxe]]

Hilda Stonebraid grew up idolizing the Soldiers of the Valandirian Mountains—their legends, their discipline, their thunderous charge echoed in every tale told around the hearth. With her bright ginger hair braided for battle and her presence equal parts beautiful and fearsome, she trained under the famed [[General Bromdir Stonehold]] himself. She lived for his stories: roaring victories, desperate last stands, and the kind of honor only won in blood and steel.

But the mountains had grown quiet. Too quiet. Their fearsome reputation kept threats at bay, leaving Hilda trapped in a life of drills, patrols, and endless routine. Glory never came, no enemy ever dared, and her dreams of battle-honed valor withered in the stillness.

So Hilda made a choice. With her axe on her back and resolve burning hotter than a forge, she slipped down the mountain and into the great city of [[Valandir]]. If the world refused to bring danger to the Valandirians, then she would go find it herself—and carve a name worthy of General Stonehold’s respect.
