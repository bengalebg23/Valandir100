[[Codex of the Scholar]]
 
 Danerius De Leon is the uncle of Hugo and part of the noble De Leon family. Danerius is a much travelled and learned man having visited numerous libraries in a quest for knowledge.  However for the last three years and at the bequest of Hugos father I have been tutoring Hugo in an attempt to guide him away from fighting skills and more towards academia. This has not been easy as Hugo is not the most attentive of student and much prefers fight school to the classroom. Although not what I had planned the thought of attending such a gathering not only lit a spark of curiously but also gave me the opportunity to practice some of the learning built up over the years.
