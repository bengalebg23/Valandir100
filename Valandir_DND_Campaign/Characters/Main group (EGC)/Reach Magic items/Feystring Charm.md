[[Minbi Drugledamp]]

Wondrous item (instrument charm), uncommon
Requires attunement by a bard

A loop of living vine and silver thread, warm to the touch. When played, the instrument’s notes seem to linger a heartbeat longer than they should.

The instrument counts as a spellcasting focus.

When Mimbi grants Bardic Inspiration, the target gains advantage on the next attack roll, ability check, or saving throw they make within the next minute.

Once per long rest, when Mimbi casts a bard spell while playing this instrument, one creature affected by the spell may move up to half its speed without provoking opportunity attacks.
