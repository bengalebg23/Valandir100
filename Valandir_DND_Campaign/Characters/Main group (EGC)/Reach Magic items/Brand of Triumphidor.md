[[Hugo de Léon]]

Brand of Triumphidor
Wondrous item (uncommon), divine blessing, requires attunement

A radiant sigil emblazons across Hugos forearm — an angular mark resembling a sword and crown. Each victory adds a faint mark to illustrate his success

**Glory Claimed**
Once per short rest, when Hugo reduces a hostile creature to 0 hit points, he may immediately claim Triumphidor’s favor:

Gain advantage on your next attack within 15seconds. This attack deals additional 1d6 radiant damage.

This effect ends early if the attack misses.

**Stand Victorious**
Once per long rest, when Hugo fails a saving throw while bloodied (below half hit points), he may instead treat the roll as a success.

When this happens, the brand burns brightly and cannot be used again until after a long rest.

**Demand for Glory**
If Hugo refuses a challenge, retreats from a clearly winnable fight, or allows another to claim a victory that was rightfully his, the brand remains dormant until he next wins a meaningful battle.
