[[Bwark]]

Wondrous Item, rare (requires attunement by a creature with proficiency in Medicine or Sleight of Hand)

A simple leather glove, soft and slightly worn. The fingers are stitched with faint silvery thread that moves slightly, as if alive. It smells faintly of tea and rain.

Bwark didn’t buy it. He didn’t craft it. Somehow it just… ended up in his bag...

**Steady Hands**
When Bwark attempts a Medicine or Sleight of Hand check, he can roll 1d6 extra and add it to the result. This bonus can be applied before or after rolling, but only once per check.

**Fumble Fix**
Once per short rest, if Bwark fails a check or attack roll by 5 or less, he can use his reaction to turn it into a success instead. The glove “nudges” his hand just right.

**Wandering Touch**
When Bwark makes a healing spell or ability check (including nonmagical medicine), he can choose a creature within 5 ft of him other than his original target and heal them for half the effect. 

It’s a tool for accidents and lucky touches, perfectly suited for Bwark: he’s never deliberate, but somehow always useful.
