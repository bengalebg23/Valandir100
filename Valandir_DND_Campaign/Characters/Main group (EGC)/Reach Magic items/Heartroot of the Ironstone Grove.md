[[Arura barkwood]]

Wondrous item, uncommon (requires attunement by a druid)

This small talisman is carved from smooth grey stone veined with living wood, as if a root grew through a mason’s work and refused to be removed. One side bears the faint geometric marks of Valandir stonecraft; the other is etched with flowing elven runes of growth and balance.

Cain would recognize the stone.
Thalira would recognize the runes.
Arura feels both.

Mechanical Effects
🌿 Rooted Balance
While attuned, Arura gains advantage on Concentration checks to maintain druid spells.
This represents her learning to steady her wild magic — grounding emotion in nature.

🪨 Stonebound Resilience (1/Long Rest)
As a reaction when Arura takes damage, she may invoke the talisman to gain resistance to that damage until the end of the triggering effect.
Visually, her skin briefly takes on the texture of stone bark — granite threaded with moss.

📜 Whisper of the Grove
Once per long rest, Arura may choose one of the following effects when she casts a druid spell of 1st level or higher:

	Gain advantage on the spell’s attack roll or saving throw DC
OR
	Treat the spell as if cast one slot level higher (maximum 3rd level)
This decision must be made before rolling.
