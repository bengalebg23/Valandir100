# 🍺 **The Stag & Sickle**

_“If it ain't loud, it ain’t lively!” — common saying among patrons_

A rowdy, sweat-scented, working-class tavern on the lower rung of Valandir’s trade district. Miners, mercenaries, low-ranked adventurers, and dockworkers pack the place every night. Violence is common, loyalty is earned, and secrets slip out between mugs of cheap ale.

---

# 🍽️ **Menu**

### **Drinks**

- **Sicklebrew Bitter** – Strong, earthy, and served in cracked clay mugs.
    
- **Red Vein Rum** – Harsh but cheap; burns like wildfire.
    
- **Twin Antler Cider** – Crisp apple cider brewed by a reclusive druid.
    
- **Adventurer’s Courage** – A spiced whiskey with a kick; popular before missions.
    

### **Food**

- **Pit-Fried Sausage Skillet** – Chorizo-like sausage, peppers, onions, skillet-fried.
    
- **Hunter’s Pie** – Thick crust stuffed with boar meat and wild herbs.
    
- **Dockside Fryplate** – Fish scraps, potatoes, and garlic butter smashed together.
    
- **Stag Jerky** – Spicy dried venison, favourite of guards on duty.

# 👥 **Key NPCs**

### **Brakka “Two-Tooth” Dunwall — Owner**

_Broad, loud, mid-40s, missing most of his teeth._  
Former pit fighter who uses his reputation to keep chaos at bay.  
He knows everyone in the trade district — and everyone owes him a favour.

**Secret:** He runs a small network of informants for a _mysterious noble_.

---

### **Lessa Quickstep — Barmaid**

_Light-footed, sharp-tongued, early 20s._  
Moves through brawls like water flowing around rocks.  
If the players treat her well, she becomes a priceless source of rumours.

---

### **Harrin Sootsleeve — Regular Patron**

_Coal miner, arms like tree trunks, surprisingly gentle._  
Claims he once saw an ambient surge underground that turned rock to glass.

---

### **The Smash Brothers (unrelated)**

_Two burly human men who “offer security” but usually start the fights._  
Names: Oggan and Rilf.  
Often available for hire as muscle.

# ⚔️ **Encounter Hooks**

### **The Fight Ring**

A hidden basement pit beneath the tavern hosts illegal brawls.  
Players can:

- Compete
    
- Bet
    
- Stop a rigged fight
    
- Discover someone fighting under duress
    

---

### **Sickle Sabotage**

Rival taverns (such as _The Worn Lantern_) are being vandalized.  
Evidence suggests the culprits drink here… but who hired them?

---

### **The Silver-Rank Fugitive**

A wounded adventurer hides upstairs.  
They have a powerful magical item and _someone_ is hunting them.  
A fight may break out if the hunters track them to the tavern.

---

### **Ambient Surge Event**

Mid-brawl, the entire tavern shakes. Bottles vibrate.  
A faint red glow pulses through the walls.  
Something beneath the district has awoken…

---

# 🧭 **Layout (Theatre of the Mind)**

- **Main Hall:** Wooden tables, long central bar, antlers and mining gear on walls.
    
- **Upper Floor:** 4–6 cheap rooms for rent. Thin walls.
    
- **Back Alley Exit:** Often used for smuggling or slipping out mid-fight.
    
- **Hidden Basement Pit:** Door behind the bar, guarded by Oggan or Rilf.
