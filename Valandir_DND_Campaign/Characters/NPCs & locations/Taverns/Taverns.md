

### 🍺 **1. The [[Worn Lantern]]**

A quiet, firelit tavern on a crooked back street. The lantern above the door is said to glow brighter when someone with strong ambient magic enters.

### 🍻 **2. The [[Stag & Sickle]]**


A rowdy, working-class establishment popular with miners, mercenaries, and low-rank adventurers. Cheap ale, loud fights, great gossip.

### 🍷 **3. [[The Velvet Thorn]]**

A high-end tavern and lounge frequented by nobles, spies, and charm-heavy adventurers. Plush seating, dim lights, and a bartender who knows everyone’s secrets.
