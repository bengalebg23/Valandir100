# 🏮 **The Worn Lantern**
_A firelit refuge on a crooked back alley of Valandir_
# **Quick Summary**
A modest, creaking tavern lit by a single ancient lantern over the door. Locals whisper that the lantern glows brighter in the presence of strong ambient magic — a harmless rumour… unless it’s true.

_Tall hooded figure who sits in the corner booth every night._

# 🔥 **Encounter Hooks**

### **Ambient Spike Event**
The Lantern above the door suddenly blazes white. Everyone panics.  
A magical surge ripples through the street — something is coming.

### **The Locked Cellar**
The players hear scraping beneath their feet. Elda goes pale and orders everyone upstairs.  
What’s down there?

### **The Vanishing Patron**
Sirran the Quiet disappears mid-meal, his cloak left behind — containing  
a **map of Valandir with strange arcane markings**

# 🧭 **Floor Map (Theatre of the Mind)**

- Main Room: 7 round tables, fireplace, long bar on the left.
- Corner Booth: Permanently occupied by Sirran. Shadowy, slightly warped floorboards.
- Back Room: Small kitchen + stairs to living quarters.
- Cellar Door: Behind the bar. Iron-bound, magically locked.
- Alley: One exit, narrow, often foggy at night.

# 🍽️ **Menu**

### **Drinks**

- **Lantern Ale** – Cheap amber ale brewed in-house. Slight smoky aftertaste.
    
- **Glowfire Mead** – Sweet elvish mead; glows faintly when shaken.
    
- **Stonewatch Stout** – Thick dwarven stout with hints of coffee and bitter chocolate.
    
- **Mage’s Breath** – A pale blue spirit served in tiny crystal cups; warms the throat like magic.
    

### **Food**

- **mushroom Stew** – variety of mushrooms, root vegetables, and red pepper. Served in a sizzling iron bowl. [[ThicketWhistle]]s favorite dish.
    
- **Lantern-Baked Loaf** – Fresh bread baked using a magically heated stone.
    
- **Herb-Crusted Boar Cutlet** – The house speciality, served with charred leeks.
    
- **Wispcakes** – Fluffy dessert cakes topped with powdered sugar; rumored to float for a second if made correctly.
