# [Vaelis Duskryn]

## Overview
Mission: Sent to Valandir to sabotage any competing teams, ensuring the Velmoran synicate is the only one able to claim the sigil 

Vaelis Duskryn is a tall elf assassin responsible for Marcus’s death. He was sent by a rival gang from another city to thin the field ahead of [Cornelius Thatch](obsidian://open?file=Valandir%20DND%20Campaign%2FCharacters%2FNPCs%20%26%20locations%2FVelmoran%2FCornelius%20Thatch.md)’s expedition.[[Act 12 - The murder]]

## Known Involvement

- Killed Marcus
- Linked to a rival gang from outside the city
- Connected to events surrounding the docklands and warehouse operations
- Was seen meeting privately with [Marcell Veyron - BCS Mob boss](obsidian://open?file=Valandir%20DND%20Campaign%2FCharacters%2FNPCs%20%26%20locations%2FBroken%20Crown%20Syndicate%2FMarcell%20Veyron%20-%20BCS%20Mob%20boss.md) shortly before Marcell’s death
- Wearing [[Amberleaf Purfume]]

## Witness Description

Witnesses described Vaelis as a tall elf who “spoke well,” seemed “too clean” and “too polite,” and carried the scent of [[Amberleaf Purfume]] described as warm and sweet, “like burnt honey.” The scent and coinage suggested a Velmoran connection.
He paid for privacy and was remembered as immediately suspicious even before violence was discovered.

## Short Backstory

Vaelis appears to be an imported specialist rather than local muscle. His role was targeted disruption, not random killing. Marcus’s death was part of a wider attempt to reduce opposition before [Cornelius Thatch](obsidian://open?file=Valandir%20DND%20Campaign%2FCharacters%2FNPCs%20%26%20locations%2FVelmoran%2FCornelius%20Thatch.md)’s expedition, suggesting planning, outside funding, and a larger criminal objective.[

His private meeting with Marcell implies he was moving through Valandir’s underworld circles before or during the killing, either as a negotiator, infiltrator, or assassin positioning the next move.

### Traits

**Fey Ancestry.** Vaelis has advantage on saving throws against being charmed, and magic can’t put him to sleep.

**Cunning Action.** On each of his turns, Vaelis can take the Dash, Disengage, or Hide action as a bonus action.

**Sneak Attack (1/Turn).** Vaelis deals an extra 14  damage when he hits a target with a finesse or ranged weapon attack and has advantage on the attack roll, or when the target is within 5 feet of one of Vaelis’s allies and Vaelis doesn’t have disadvantage.

**Elegant Killer.** If Vaelis speaks with a creature for at least 1 minute outside combat, he has advantage on his first Deception or Persuasion check made against that creature during the encounter.

**Unnerving Composure.** Vaelis has advantage on saving throws against being frightened.

### Actions

**Multiattack.** Vaelis makes two attacks with his shortsword or hand crossbow.

**Shortsword.** _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target.  
_Hit:_ 8  piercing damage plus 7  poison damage.

**Hand Crossbow.** _Ranged Weapon Attack:_ +7 to hit, range 30/120 ft., one target.  
_Hit:_ 7  piercing damage plus 7  poison damage.

**Silencing Blade (Recharge 5–6).** Vaelis makes one shortsword attack. On a hit, the target must succeed on a DC 14 Constitution saving throw or be unable to speak above a whisper until the end of Vaelis’s next turn.

### Reactions

**Slip Aside.** When a creature Vaelis can see misses him with an attack, he may move up to 10 feet without provoking opportunity attacks from that creature.

### Bonus Actions

**Poised Withdrawal.** Vaelis takes the Disengage action and gains advantage on his next Stealth check before the end of his turn.
