[[Act 14 - The Gala]]
## **Shop Name:** The Verdant Ledger

**Location:** A cluttered but charming store tucked into a side street in Valandir’s market district, with creeping ivy along its roof and a faint scent of herbs and leather. Outside hangs a carved wooden sign: _“The Verdant Ledger – For the Prepared Adventurer.”_

**Owner:** **Milo Thatch**, a wiry man in his late 50s with sharp, curious eyes that constantly dart around the room. His long, graying hair is tied back in a loose braid, and his hands are perpetually stained with ink from ledger entries and potion tests. Milo is a retired explorer who now catalogs rare and practical items for adventurers. He’s part eccentric, part meticulous, and loves giving advice—even if it comes with an amusing anecdote or cryptic warning.

**Personality quirks:**

- Talks to inanimate objects as if they were people (“Ah, yes, the rope—don’t think I didn’t notice that fray.”)
    
- Occasionally forgets a sale mid-sentence but will immediately make it right with a discount or a free sample.
    
- Offers cryptic hints about dangers in the jungle, e.g., _“Watch the purple-leafed shrubs; not everything that glimmers is a gem.”_
