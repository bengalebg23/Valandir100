## **1. Valandir Armoury: “The Iron Griffon”**

**Description:**  
Nestled in the bustling Artisan district of Valandir, **The Iron Griffon** is a tall, stone-fronted building with a carved griffon clutching a sword over the doorway. Inside, polished steel and brass gleam under lantern light. Racks of swords, axes, crossbows, and shields line the walls. Each weapon has been curated for adventurers seeking quality and sometimes, rarity.

**Owner:**  
**[[Master Lorian Veylin]]**, a middle-aged human with soot-streaked hands and a perpetual scowl softened only by pride in his stock. Lorian is meticulous and values craftsmanship above all else, but he’s willing to haggle with adventurers who prove they know their weapons. He has a fondness for stories of daring adventures, which can sway him to give discounts or special items.

**Key Features:**

- Rows of melee and ranged weapons, from standard iron swords to exotic polearms.
    
- A small section of enchanted weapons for higher-tier adventurers.
    
- Lockable display cases for particularly valuable or unique weapons.
    
- A leather-bound ledger with a “special orders” section.
    

**Sample Inventory & Prices (in gold pieces):**

- Longsword: 15 gp
    
- Warhammer: 20 gp
    
- Crossbow: 25 gp
    
- Arrows (20): 2 gp
    
- +1 Weapon (basic magical): 300 gp
    
- Masterwork Exotic Weapon (e.g., spiked chain, glaive): 150 gp

## **2. Valandir Smithy: “Molten Anvil”**

**Description:**  
Across the street from the armoury, **Molten Anvil** is a large, open-fronted smithy. The air is warm and thick with smoke, and the rhythmic hammering of molten metal rings out day and night. Heat from the furnaces causes the air to shimmer slightly.

**Owner/Smith:**  
**Tharun Emberhand**, a burly dwarf with streaks of grey in his beard. He’s quiet, precise, and somewhat gruff, but he can forge almost anything given the right materials and time. Tharun loves magical commissions and enjoys experimenting with integrating enchantments into armour or weapons.

**Key Features:**

- Multiple forges, bellows, and anvils.
    
- A display rack of recently completed armour and weapons.
    
- Magical runes etched into walls to enhance enchanting work.
    
- A small shop area where adventurers can commission custom pieces.
    

**Bespoke Plate Armour Example:**

- **Description:** Full plate of finely crafted steel, custom-fitted, adorned with subtle etchings. The magical property: **“Ward of Flame”** – wearer gains resistance to fire damage (half damage from fire sources).
    
- **Price:** 1,200 gp (base plate: 600 gp, magical property: 600 gp)
    
- **Time to Craft:** 6-8 weeks (in-game, can be compressed or extended for plot purposes)
