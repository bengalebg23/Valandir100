[[armoury or Smithy in Valandir]]
A middle-aged human with soot-streaked hands and a perpetual scowl softened only by pride in his stock. Lorian is meticulous and values craftsmanship above all else, but he’s willing to haggle with adventurers who prove they know their weapons. He has a fondness for stories of daring adventures, which can sway him to give discounts or special items.
