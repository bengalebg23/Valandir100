[[Jacob Ravensbane]]
[[Hugo de Léon]]

## Letter 1

Hugo,

Hello. I am writing this because Miss Alder says writing helps you put your thoughts in the right order. I hope my spelling is better now. I am trying hard.
Thank you again for letting me stay here. I know I say that a lot but I mean it every time.

The bed is very soft. It is strange not sharing with others to stay warm. I still wake up early but there is no one to nudge.

From,
Jacob

## Letter 2
Hello Hugo,

I am still trying to do well.

The letters are harder than I thought. The other boys read fast. They don’t move their finger along the page. I try not to, but then I lose my place.

I am practising at night so I can be quicker but it makes me tired in the mornings. I will get it right. I promise.

From,
Jacob

## Letter 3
Hi Hugo,

I hope you get these letters. Miss Alder says you have gone away.

I'm learning numbers. They are easier. I like when they add up properly. That feels good because they stay where you put them.

I helped carry the coal without being asked. And I remembered to clean my boots before coming inside today. I am trying to remember all the right ways of doing things.

I don’t want you to think you chose badly.

From,
Jacob

## Letter 4
Hi Hugo,

I'm worried about you. Miss Alder says you have been gone for too long.

I know I don’t know as much as the others yet, but I am learning. I listen very carefully. I will work harder if I need to. I can wake up earlier if that helps.

Thank you for giving me this chance. I won’t waste it.

Please come back soon.
Jacob

## Letter 5 
Hi Hugo,

Miss Alder and the other grown ups seem worried too. I hope you are okay.

The boys here are very clever. They know words I have not heard before and they laugh because I say them wrong. I am practising the long ones so I don’t slow anyone down.

They say mean things to me sometimes. Mrs Alder moved my seat closer to the front so I can see better. It is easier there.

I hope you write soon. 
Jacob
