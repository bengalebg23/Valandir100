[[Act 14 - The Gala]]

# Lord Pell Dawnwick
*Minor Noble — Unwitting Amberleaf Decoy*

## Appearance
Portly, cheerful human man in his 60s. An aggressively expensive coat in burgundy and gold that he is very proud of. Silver buttons. A cologne so warm and sweet it arrives in a room before he does — burnt honey, unmistakably [[Amberleaf Purfume]]. He has been wearing it proudly all evening and cannot understand why it seems to be attracting unusual attention.

## The Decoy
[[Vaelis Duskryn]] anonymously delivered a complimentary bottle of Amberleaf to Pell's townhouse this morning with a card suggesting it was a gift from an admirer. Pell assumed a lady of quality had taken notice of him. He applied it generously.

He has absolutely no idea he is a distraction. He is completely harmless. He is delighted to be here.

## Personality
- Warm, enthusiastic, socially oblivious
- Loves talking about his prize-winning hounds (Bramble, Duchess, and the late great Fortnum)
- Genuinely kind in the way of someone who has never had a difficult day
- Will talk to anyone who makes eye contact for longer than two seconds
- Has been trying to find someone to discuss the hound situation with all evening

## Purpose at the Gala
Any player tracking the Amberleaf scent across the room will find Pell instead of Vaelis. This is working exactly as intended by someone Pell has never met and will never know about.

## Hook
> *"Magnificent evening isn't it! Someone sent me the most extraordinary cologne this morning — Velmoran I'm told! Do you know I've never even been to Velmoran — always meant to. Do you hunt? I have the most remarkable pair of scent hounds, Bramble and Duchess, absolute champions both, though I have to say since Fortnum passed the dynamic has shifted somewhat..."*

## If Asked About the Cologne
> *"Anonymous gift! Can you imagine. Arrived this morning with a rather elegant card — no signature. I assumed Lady Harwick but she hasn't acknowledged it so perhaps not. Lovely scent though don't you think? Very warm. Like burnt honey."*

He will produce the card if asked. It is entirely blank except for the words *"With compliments."* Written in a hand none of the party recognise.

## What He Knows
Nothing relevant. Everything about hounds.

## Connections
- [[Vaelis Duskryn]] — used him without his knowledge
- Lady Harwick — suspected admirer (incorrect)
- Bramble, Duchess, the late Fortnum — his hounds
