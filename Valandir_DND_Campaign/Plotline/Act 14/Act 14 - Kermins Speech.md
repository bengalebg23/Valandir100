[[Act 14 - The Gala]]

[[Kermin Traylow]] rises slowly from his seat, the great hall quieting as the deep voice of the old dragonborn carries across the room.
“Friends of Valandir… tonight we gather in the shadow of difficult days.”
He pauses, letting the room settle.
“Our city has endured turmoil — rifts in the fabric of magic itself, and the loss of brave souls who stood against it. We mourn them, and we honour the courage that kept our home standing.”
His gaze shifts toward the party.
“In times such as these, wisdom demands patience. The Library and the Guild have worked tirelessly to understand the forces at play, and through that work a path forward has begun to emerge.”
A small nod.
“It is not a single victory that secures a city — but the steady work of many hands, many minds, and many hearts.”
He gestures toward the party.
“Tonight we recognise those who stepped forward when Valandir needed them most. Their actions have helped turn uncertainty into progress, and given this city the time it needs to heal.”
He raises his glass.
“To those we lost… to those who stood firm… and to the future of Valandir.”
“May it be steadier than the days behind us.”
