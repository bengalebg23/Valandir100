Helios doesn't come to the gala. He's smarter than that — a mob boss crashing a civic celebration is bad optics.

Instead, he sends a message through the gala. 

One of the dignitaries — a city merchant Helios owns — quietly pulls Lady Hollingsworth aside during the drinks reception. 

Within minutes, the mood around the party shifts. Hollingsworth looks at them differently. The message is clear: Helios has told the right people that the party lied to him, presented a stranger's head as Garrus, and are now marked.

He isn't threatening violence at the gala — he's just closing every door in the city behind them.
