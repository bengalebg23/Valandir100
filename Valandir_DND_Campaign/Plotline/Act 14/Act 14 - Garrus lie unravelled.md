[[Act 14 - The Gala]]

### Garrus — the lie unravels**
The team visited the Adventurers' Guild and spoke to Lady Hollingsworth. They pressed her to get Garrus out of the city immediately, warning that the BCS had him in their sights. She agreed to send envoys to locate him and arrange his removal.

However, Garrus — being Garrus — was too drunk in a tavern to be quietly spirited away. He made a scene, drawing attention to himself. Helios' men happened to be nearby, saw him very much alive, and reported back to Helios. The fake head ruse has been exposed. Helios now knows the party lied to him.
