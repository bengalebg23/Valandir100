### At Initiative Count 20 (every round)
Roll 1d6:
1 — Civilian Panic
Crowd surges
All movement = difficult terrain this round
2 — Table Flip
Nearby table overturned
Creates half cover
3 — Flying Bottle 🍺
Random creature makes DEX save (DC 12)
Fail = 1d4 damage + disadvantage next attack
4 — Bouncer Returns (fun tie-in 👀)
Someone tries to break up fight
Grapples nearest person
5 — Fire Hazard
Lantern knocked over
Small fire starts
Area becomes dangerous next round
6 — Guard Whistle 🚨
City watch incoming in 2 rounds
👉 Forces urgency
