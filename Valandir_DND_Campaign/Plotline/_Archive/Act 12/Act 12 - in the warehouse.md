[[Act 12 - The docklands]]
In the warehouse there are a huge number of crates and several patrolling guards. 

### Entrance 
Stacks of cargo:
- Timber crates
- Barrels of salt fish
- Imported cloth
- Wine casks
- Dock paperwork:
- Shipping manifests
- Trade stamps from other cities


### 1. The Front Area – Legitimate Cover
When they first sneak in, it looks mostly normal.
Stacks of cargo:
Timber crates
Barrels of salt fish
Imported cloth
Wine casks
Dock paperwork:
Shipping manifests
Trade stamps from other cities
This establishes the warehouse as a legitimate trading front.
But something feels too organised and too guarded.

### 2. Hidden Operations (BCS Smuggling)
Behind stacked crates or beneath a trapdoor they find the real operation.
#### Weapons Cache
- Large crates stamped with fake trade markings contain:
- Crossbows and bolts
- Daggers and short swords
- Clubs and cudgels
- Leather armour
- This suggests Helios is arming thugs across the city.
#### Gunpowder Store
- A locked iron-banded chest containing:
- Powder kegs
- Grenade-style glass firebombs
- Slow match fuses
- This hints the syndicate is preparing for urban violence or sabotage.

#### Illegal Imports
- Hidden compartments contain:
- Feywild narcotics
- Dream-dust (hallucinogenic powder)
- Black lotus resin
- This gives you drug trade flavour without needing heavy mechanics.

#### 3. The Real Interesting Stuff (Plot Hooks)
This is where the story lives.
- Syndicate Ledger
- A coded book listing:
- Protection payments
- Businesses Helios has shut down
- Casino takeovers
- Bribes to city officials
If decoded it reveals how deep BCS control runs.
#### Message From Helios
A sealed letter or note:
"The Guild grows weak. The city belongs to those willing to take it."
This reinforces his open war mindset.

#### A Strange Crate
This is a fun twist option.
Inside they find something unexpected:
A magical artifact being transported
A strange key fragment
Or something linked to the Eccentric Entrepreneur's quest

#### Optional Twist (Very Good for Tension)
The warehouse might not be abandoned.
Possible encounters:
2–4 dock guards playing cards
A foreman accountant counting coin
A hidden lookout on the rafters
This keeps stealth tense without forcing combat.
