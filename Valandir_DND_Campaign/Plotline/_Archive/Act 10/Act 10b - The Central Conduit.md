# Captain Crowe, Warden of the Valve
Medium humanoid (human), Lawful Neutral (fracturing)
CR: ~8 (solo boss with terrain)
## Armor Class
18 (reinforced officer’s coat, arcane buckler)
Hit Points
135 (18d8 + 54)
(Phase thresholds at 90 HP and 45 HP)
Speed
30 ft.
## Ability Scores
STR 14 (+2)
DEX 16 (+3)
CON 16 (+3)
INT 14 (+2)
WIS 14 (+2)
CHA 16 (+3)

## Saving Throws
Dex +7, Con +7, Wis +6

## Skills
Athletics +6, Insight +6, Intimidation +7, Arcana +6
## Resistances
Force; advantage on saves vs spells while within the conduit chamber
## Senses
Passive Perception 12
## Languages
Common, Dwarvish

## Traits
Tactical Commander
Once per round, Crowe can issue a command as a reaction:
- **An ally within 30 ft can move up to half its speed or make one weapon attack.**
(In the final fight, this mostly represents environmental control or pre-placed defenders.)
- **Arcane Buckler (3/day)**
When hit by an attack, Crowe gains +4 AC against that attack and the triggering attack misses.

## Desperate Focus
The first time Crowe would drop to 0 HP, he instead drops to 1 HP and immediately enters Phase 3.

ACTIONS
## Multiattack
Crowe makes two attacks with his Arcane Rapier or one attack and uses Valve Control.

**Arcane Rapier**
Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 5 ft.
Hit: 9 (1d8 + 5) piercing + 7 (2d6) force damage.

**Arcane Shot**
Ranged Spell Attack: +7 to hit, range 60 ft.
Hit: 13 (3d8) force damage, and the target’s speed is reduced by 10 ft until the end of their next turn

## **Valve Control (Recharge 5–6)**
Crowe manipulates the conduit.
Choose one:
**Force Surge**: All creatures in a 15-ft radius must make a DC 15 Dex save or take 14 (4d6) force damage, half on success.
**Gravitic Pull**: Creatures are pulled 10 ft toward the crystal spine unless they succeed on a DC 15 Str save.

# PHASE CHANGES
## PHASE 1 — The Officer (135–91 HP)
- Crowe fights defensively
- Uses positioning and reactions
- Attempts to disable, not kill
- Uses Valve Control sparingly
- Roleplay: Calm, clipped orders, professional.

## **PHASE** 2 — The Engineer (90–46 HP)
- Environmental Effect (Lair-like):
- At initiative 20, one surge occurs:
- Arcane sparks (difficult terrain)
- Crystal shard eruption (Dex save or 1d10 force)
- Magic interference (disadvantage on spell attack rolls until next round)
**New Action (1/turn):**
- Overcharge
- Crowe overloads his gear:
- All creatures within 10 ft take 10 (3d6) force damage
- Crowe also takes 5 force damage
- Roleplay: Raised voice, justification, frustration.
- 
## PHASE 3 — The Breaking Point (45–0 HP)
Crowe is bleeding magic.
Changes:
Loses Arcane Buckler
Loses reactions
AC drops to 16
Damage from Arcane Rapier increases by +3
Last Pull (1/encounter)
As a bonus action, Crowe opens the valve one final notch.
Massive surge threatens the chamber

Party must choose:
Stop him (damage)
Stabilize the valve (Arcana / Athletics / Tools checks)
Absorb the surge (mushroom synergy, resistance, consequences)
This decides the ending.
Roleplay: Exhausted, furious, resolute.
