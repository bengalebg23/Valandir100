[[Act 11b - next steps]]

As you rest near the jungle totem, the crystal you carry begins to hum softly in your hand. When you touch it, your vision shifts: the Fey realm stretches out before you, frozen mid-motion — trees hung in the wind, streams halted, creatures paused.
Through the stillness, you see your family, trapped in stasis, unable to move or speak. Acting instinctively, you channel the crystal’s energy — a pulse of light flows from it. For a brief moment, your family moves, breathes, smiles, whispering their thanks… then they freeze again.
In that instant, it becomes clear: the crystal lattice cavern has halted the magic. The rift that once let Fey energy flow is locked. You realize you have a choice:
Open the rift — the frozen magic would return, freeing your family, but it will damage the city or cavern and may draw unwanted attention.
Leave the rift closed — your family remains frozen, safe but trapped, and the city remains intact.
The crystal pulses in your hand, as if waiting for your decision.
