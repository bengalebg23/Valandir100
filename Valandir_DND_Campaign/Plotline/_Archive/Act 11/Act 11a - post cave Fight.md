## STEP 1 — The Surge (Resolution Without Another Fight)
Crowe falls.
There is no explosion.
There is a growing hum.
Low at first, building, the crystals overhead begin to resonate, become in tune with each other, small cracks form and shafts of light ignite from them. 
Then the veins throughout the chamber and ravine ignite. Illuminating the chamber. Finally it peaks. It begins to reach a crescendo

The vent opens
But it does not cause a rupture
The energy releases as a shockwave

Describe it like this:
A pulse moves outward through the ravine. The crystal veins flare white. The mushrooms across the cavern glow in response. Then a wave — not of force — but of pressure. Like a deep breath being exhaled.
Cut to the city above:
Windows rattle.
Dust falls from rafters.
Bleached plants flicker with colour for a moment.
 reflect faint prismatic light for a few hours

Then:
Silence.
The vent hums steadily.
The crisis is contained.

## STEP 2 — Escape the Caves (Skill Challenge)
Now the real danger:
The earth adjusts.
Cracks shift. Stone grinds. Dust clouds rise.
Not a collapse.
But unstable.
5 Successes Before 3 Failures
Let them narrate how they help:
- Athletics: holding beams
- Acrobatics: navigating broken ledges
- Survival: fastest route upward
- Arcana: sensing where magic is weakest
- Spells = auto success but resource cost
- On a failure:
- Minor falling damage
- Separation for a moment
- Someone nearly drops into a fissure
Make it fast-paced. Keep it tense but not lethal.

### Mimbi’s Crystal Moment
Mid-escape:
The cracked crystal in her pocket begins to vibrate.
It is resonating with the stabilised vent.

Options:
She holds it (Arcana check to stabilise)
She drops it (it embeds in stone and grows faint threads)
She smashes it (burst of harmless prismatic dust)
If stabilised: It becomes inert.
If embedded: It becomes a tiny permanent crystal growth in the cave.
If smashed: She gets a faint Fey scar across her palm.

None catastrophic.
Just consequence.

### Lyra’s Arrival
As they reach a final unstable chamber:
A flash of blue light.
Lyra appears.
Not fully calm.
Hair static with arcane discharge.
She shouts:
“Now would be an excellent time!”
Teleport circle opens.
They step through as the tunnel groans behind them.
No long debate.
Just urgency.

## *STEP 3 — The Library*
Hard tonal shift.
Quiet.
Stillness.
Scholars whispering.
Kermin waiting.
He already knows.
Because the pulse rolled through the city.
He looks… older.
Relieved.
He says:
“You have done what we could not decide to do.”
He does not praise loudly.
He acknowledges.
That feels more earned.
Rewards
Keep it clean and political.
- 1,000–1,500 gp each (or appropriate scale)
- A townhouse in Valandir (shared or individual apartments)
- Formal advisory status to the AG and Library
- Access to restricted stacks
This is elevation.
They are no longer field operatives.
They are recognised stabilisers.

> "We will send scholars into the caves, we will push the research into the mushrooms."
> 
In one week from now, A celebratory gala will be held in honor of the fallen, to celebrate their sacrifice and to bring life back to the city. You will be guests of honor



Final Image to End Session
As they leave the Library:
The city is quiet.
Bleached vines on a wall begin slowly regaining green at the edges.
Not fully healed.
But alive.
Far beneath the city:
The vent hums steadily.
And in the Feywild…
The pressure is gone.
Not fixed.
But balanced.
Cut there.
