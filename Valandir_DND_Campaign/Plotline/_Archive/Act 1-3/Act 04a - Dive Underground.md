## THE BREACH BELOW
Strange pulses of ambient magic have been detected beneath Valandir. These don’t match natural random surges, they feel structured, planned, organized. 

[[Captain Crowe]] sends a number of iron parties to investigate a forgotten maintenance network below the city — tunnels built to investigate below the city. Looking for mana springs (a pool of raw energy). 

Deep inside, a researcher named [[Dr. Mavien Korliss]] has been conducting illegal experiments:
- Tapping into ambient magic
- Trying to “stabilize” it
- Actually weakening the boundaries that keep surge-beasts dormant

Her intentions are scientific, not malicious…
But she is incredibly dangerous.
