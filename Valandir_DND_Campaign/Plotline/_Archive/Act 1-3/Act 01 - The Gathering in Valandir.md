ACT 1 — “The Gathering in Valandir”---

# Scene01_1 Arrival + The Mass Recruitment
Opening vibe

Drop the players right into the heart of the capital’s chaos and anticipation.

Read-aloud flavour (adjust to taste):
Valandir’s towers glimmer in the afternoon haze, threads of stray magic drifting like heatwaves between rooftops. Ahead, the immense stone courtyard of the Adventurers Guild vibrates with noise—hundreds of hopefuls packed shoulder to shoulder, recruits from every corner of Dinah answering the urgent call. Talk of an overdue monster surge hangs in the air like a storm cloud.

---

What’s happening right now

- The Adventurers Guild is doing an emergency intake.
- Normally they onboard maybe 20–30 per week.
- Today? 100–150 recruits, some terrified, some buzzing with excitement.
- The city knows a monster surge is imminent, because:
- ambient magic spikes have been increasing
- mana pressure readings are off the charts
- scouts have vanished from the outer wilds
- the sky has had three “false auroras” in the past week

This gives a perfect reason for the giant gathering and the emphasis on ranks.

---

Scene Layout — The Courtyard

- Picture a wide rectangular stone courtyard at the front of the AG complex:
- Wooden stages being erected
- Banners announcing the “Emergency Recruitment Window”
- Senior adventurers pacing like drill sergeants
- Fresh recruits clustering into little cliques
- A few magical anomalies sparking because emotions are high (stray sparks of arcane flare up from the crowd)
- Bronze adventurers walk the perimeter acting as marshals. Their auras pulse faintly and can be retracted.
- Only 2–3 Silver members stand elevated on a balcony. Their auras are disciplined, sharp, and controlled like a blade’s edge.
- No Gold present — they’re fighting “more important threats.”

Important moment

As the players step in, a Gold-ranked instructor walks through the crowd, and the entire courtyard falls silent in a wave as people feel his aura before they see him.

Recruit next to the party whispers:

> “That… that pressure? That means Gold. You don’t wanna annoy a Gold.”

Instant worldbuilding with no exposition dump.


---

The Inciting Event (optional but cool)

- Right as orientation is about to begin:
- The sky rumbles even though there’s no storm
- For one second, the light in the courtyard goes dim
- Magical instruments carried by AG staff begin howling, reacting to something out in the wilds
- Someone in the crowd mutters:

> “Another spike… that’s the fourth this week…”

The crowd gets restless. People shove. Magic sputters. The tension is thick — and your players are standing in the middle of 150 half-panicked wannabe adventurers.

Then the Guild Captain steps onto the stage.
--

Introduce Key NPC — Guild Captain [[Lady Hollingsworth]]

Calm, authoritative, commands the entire courtyard instantly

Her speech could go like:

> “Recruits! The surge is coming. You’re here because we need every able body to prepare. Some of you will pass the assessment. Some of you won’t.
> 
> You’ll be ranked today, trained over the coming weeks and deployed as needed to protect the citizens of Valandir and beyond. 
> 
> Some of you are new to adventuring, monsters and the power that comes with the Guild. 
> Some of you have tried for years to gain entry but have simply not shown the attributes we require 
> Some here have naturally made it past the threshold to Iron rank through hard work,  determination, or shortcuts (glance at the thugs)
> 
> Do not think because the surge is imminent we will dilute the requirements of entry. You will be our ambassadors and we only accept the best.
> 
> Finally I will wish you all Good luck and welcome to the Adventurers Guild of Valandir.”



Behind her is a scruffy instructor [[Captain Crowe]] who is gruff and speaks plainly.

"Today is about showing what you can do and the skills you have acquired so far! We are not looking for outright power, we need tactical thinkers, teamwork and skills that will keep both yourselves and civilians alive! 

You have all been given a coloured badge on arrival #OrangeGamma. Find your destination on the board and make your way to the appropriate trial area...
