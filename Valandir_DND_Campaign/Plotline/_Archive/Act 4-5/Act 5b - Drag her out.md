
[[Dr. Mavien Korliss]]
[[Act 5 - overview]]

**They Drag Her Out — Lab Collapses**

The party prioritises safety and legality.
Her Reaction
When they restrain her:
	“If they find this — they’ll shut it down!
	You don’t understand — this is the only way to FIX the surges!”
She panics not because of danger — but exposure.

As she’s dragged away:
- Crystals fracture
- Rifts widen
- The lab begins collapsing into a magical sinkhole

The escape is frantic.
Above ground, she is shaking — and realises the consequences.
“…They’re going to come for me.”
This is the moment she truly understands she’s crossed a line.

**AG Reaction**
The Adventure Society response is severe but controlled.
- Containment officers arrive.
- Arcane inspectors.
- Paperwork. Guards.

AG Marshal:
	“Dr. Mavien Korliss.
	You are accused of unauthorised ambient manipulation beneath Valandir.”
She does not deny it.

The party is questioned:
- Why did you remove her?
- Did you know it was illegal?
- Why wasn’t the Society informed sooner?

Because they acted to stop it: → Party is protected
→ Mog receives a stern warning
→ No punishment, but reputational caution

Dr. Korliss is:
- Detained
- Placed under investigation
- Not imprisoned (yet)
