*Welcome to Valandir*

Valandir is the shining capital city of Dinah, a continent much like medieval Europe, surrounded by many nations but unified under Valandir’s influence. It is the political, cultural, and magical centre of the world.

---

 *A World of Surging Magic* 

Dinah is steeped in ambient magic. It’s everywhere — in the air, the soil, the water. Most of the time it lies dormant, but at unpredictable moments it coalesces or spikes in power, causing strange and dangerous events:

1. Ordinary objects sometimes awaken into magical items.

2. Animals — and even people — can warp into feral monstrosities and aberrations, attacking anything they encounter.

Every few years the ambient magic erupts in a massive Surge, unleashing waves of new monsters across the continent.

---

 *Life in Valandir* 

Valandir is filled with ordinary folk — labourers, merchants, artisans — most of whom live humble lives, scraping by while dreaming of something more.
To them, magical power is the ultimate prize: a ticket to higher status and better pay, especially through the dangerous but lucrative profession of monster hunting.

---

 *Two major institutions shape life in Valandir:* 

1. The Adventure Society
A powerful, respected organisation of skilled adventurers.
Members take on monster-hunting contracts, protect the public during Surges, and often serve the needs of nobles and political leaders.
Becoming a member means status, coin, and glory — but only for those strong enough to earn it.

2. The Grand Library
A colossal, heavily protected repository of history, arcane knowledge, and scholarly secrets.
It is run by the enigmatic, near-religious order of Librarians, mages dedicated to preserving and expanding the world’s knowledge.
Their influence rivals kings, and their motives are often mysterious.

---

 *A Surge Is Coming* 

Dinah is long overdue for another magic Surge. Tensions rise, monsters grow restless, and the need for trained adventurers has never been higher.
Recruitment campaigns have swept across the continent, calling for anyone with talent — or courage — to step forward.

---

Where Our Story Begins

You stand in the training compound of the Adventure Society, surrounded by 100–150 other hopeful recruits.
Today marks the beginning of the entrance trials — tests designed to separate the exceptional from the ordinary.

Only the strongest, smartest, or most resourceful will earn a place… and survive what comes next.
