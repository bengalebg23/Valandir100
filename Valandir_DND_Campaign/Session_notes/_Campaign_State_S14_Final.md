# Soren Mourne — Introduction & Foreshadowing
## Three Beats Before the First Meeting

---

### Beat 1 — A Stranger Asking Questions
*Timing: Shortly after the warehouse fight*

**Darric the bouncer** — still hiding at the Coaching Inn by the south gate — gets a visit. A well-dressed man. Polite, academic, no threats. He asks about the EGC. Where they drink. Where they sleep. Who their contacts are. He listens carefully, thanks Darric, and leaves a card.

*Soren Mourne. Velmoran Institute of Applied Arcana.*

Darric sends word to the party through a street contact — nervous, not sure if he did the right thing telling them.

**What this establishes:**
- Soren is already in Valandir
- He is methodical and patient
- He is not hiding who he is — that's almost more unsettling than if he were

---

### Beat 2 — Lady Hollingsworth
*Timing: When the party visit the Guild to arrange crystal distribution*

Hollingsworth mentions a Velmoran academic visited the Guild the previous day. Asked about the EGC's recent activities — specifically the warehouse fight. She told him nothing but she is clearly uncomfortable.

*"He was very well informed about things he shouldn't know. He didn't threaten me. He didn't need to."*

She hands the party his card. Same card.

**What this establishes:**
- He is closing in methodically through the party's known contacts
- He has intelligence sources in Valandir already
- He is giving the party a chance to come to him before he escalates

---

### Beat 3 — The Note
*Timing: The party return to the townhouse or wherever they are based*

A sealed letter on the table. No forced entry. Nobody saw anyone. The seal is Velmoran academic — a stylised crystal inside a circle.

Inside, one line in precise handwriting:

*"I believe you have something that belongs to my research. I would very much like to discuss its return. — S.M."*

He could have done something. He didn't. He is telling them he could have.

**What this establishes:**
- He has access to wherever they sleep
- He is still offering negotiation over confrontation
- The party are now afraid of a man they have never met

---

### The First Meeting
*Timing: Party's choice — they can seek him out or wait for him to find them*

Soren suggests somewhere neutral. A library reading room, a quiet tavern, somewhere academic. He arrives alone — apparently. He is precise, warm in an unsettling way, and completely certain.

He makes his offer first:

*"The crystals you have secured represent three years of acquisition work and a significant investment by the Velmoran Institute. I am authorised to offer you fair compensation for their return and safe passage out of Valandir."*

He listens to their counter-arguments carefully. Takes notes. Does not agree. Does not get angry.

On the bleaching:
*"I understand your concern for the Feywild. It is a legitimate concern. It is also a separate problem from the one I am solving."*

On crystal instability:
*"All new energy sources carry risk. That is what research is for."*

He will not be moved today. But he remembers everything they say.

### 🔴 3. Soren Mourne — Incoming Threat
Senior Research Director, Velmoran Institute of Applied Arcana. Instigator of the crystal acquisition programme. Has been in Valandir waiting for confirmation his shipment left. When it doesn't arrive he starts looking for the party.

**Who he is:**
- Not a criminal in his own mind — a scientist with a mandate
- Sold the ley line energy programme to Velmoran senior leadership
- Convinced leadership that bottled crystal energy = enchantment industry, military superiority, energy independence
- Has stretched his mandate significantly and cannot afford to fail
- Methodical, patient, precise — will negotiate before escalating
- Fatal flaw: too invested in being right to process contradictory evidence

**His introduction — three foreshadowing beats:**
1. Darric the bouncer gets a visit — Soren asking questions, leaves a card
2. Lady Hollingsworth — he visited the Guild, well informed, not threatening
3. A note appears in the party's base — *"I believe you have something that belongs to my research. — S.M."*

**Appearance:** Late fifties, tall, slightly stooped, grey hair pulled back, immaculate Velmoran academic robes with crystal-powered details. Carries a leather satchel of notes. Looks like a professor.

**The argument that eventually lands on him — Mimbi's line:**
*"The crystals don't store the energy. They ARE the conduit. You haven't been bottling power. You've been blocking it. When the source is gone your gauntlet goes dark and takes your hand with it."*

- **Status:** In Valandir, gathering information. Foreshadowing begins after warehouse fight.
