## Active Threads (Priority Order)

### 🔴 1. Helios & the BCS — Immediate Danger
- **Helios** is the new leader of the Broken Crown Syndicate (BCS). Ruthless, calculating, expanding aggressively.
- The party framed **Garrus Vanton** for a warehouse break-in and presented a fake severed head to Helios as proof of his death.
- **Garrus was subsequently seen alive and drunk in a tavern.** Helios's men witnessed this. The lie is exposed.
- Helios has sent word to Lady Hollingsworth at the Adventurers' Guild that the party's presence in Valandir is a personal inconvenience. Doors are closing.
- Helios also wants answers on his father **Marcell Veyron's** murder (see thread 2).
- **Status:** The party does not yet know Helios knows about Garrus. This is expected to come to light at the Gala.

### 🔴 2. Bwark's Exposure — Immediate Danger
- Bwark treated Marcell Veyron at the Hellbrand Casino on the night Marcell died in the street.
- **Serena Silmore** (casino owner, in Helios's pocket) has identified Bwark as the healer.
- At the Gala, Bwark received a note: *"The healer who treated Marcell Veyron is known to us. Come to the east corridor side entrance before the speeches end. Come alone. — A friend of Serena Silmore."*
- Bwark has not yet gone to that meeting.
- **Status:** Ticking clock. Helios likely already knows via Serena.

### 🟠 3. Vaelis Duskryn — Active Threat
- Tall elf assassin from **Velmoran**. Killed Marcell Veyron using an exotic magical poison.
- His mission: eliminate competing teams ahead of **Cornelius Thatch's** expedition to recover the Verdant Sigil.
- Identified via Amberleaf perfume (warm, like burnt honey — a Velmoran tell) and Velmoran coin.
- The party investigated Highspire Manor — found the room recently vacated. Partially burned notes. A crossed-out list of names (Marcell is one). Glimpsed Vaelis escaping across rooftops.
- **At the Gala:** Vaelis is present in disguise as a Velmoran trade attaché. Different hair, different clothes, no Amberleaf. Back to the wall. Hasn't touched his drink. Watching Cornelius. Watching the party.
- **Status:** At large. The party has not yet connected Vaelis directly to Cornelius.

### 🟠 4. Cornelius Thatch — The Expedition Hook
- Velmoran merchant and expedition sponsor. Tall, impeccably dressed, precise, warm, faintly mischievous.
- Sponsored the Gala. Has been watching the party all evening.
- Is aware the party's situation in Valandir is becoming untenable (Helios).
- Has pitched the party in a private alcove during the Gala:
  - A jungle on an unmapped island, two weeks by sea
  - An ancient totem — the **Verdant Sigil** — inside a ruin guarded by a territorial apex predator (a giant Chameleon with aerosolised poison)
  - The Sigil is a calibration key to **the Vault of Khar'zeth**, an ancient pre-imperial treasury
  - Two other teams already assembled. A third team (the party) needed.
  - **Contract: 10% of everything recovered**
  - Departs in four days
- Cornelius does NOT know (or claims not to know) that Vaelis has been eliminating rivals on behalf of the Velmoran syndicate.
- **Status:** Pitch made. Party has not yet committed. This is the end of Session 13 / start of Session 14 decision point.

### 🟡 5. Garrus Vanton — Unresolved
- NPC introduced Session 1. Top-knot. Loud. Part of a rival adventuring group (drinks at The Broken Keel).
- The party framed him to Helios for the warehouse break-in. Fake head presented. Lie now exposed.
- Lady Hollingsworth at the AG was asked to get Garrus out of the city. She sent envoys but Garrus was too drunk in a tavern and made a scene — Helios's men saw him alive.
- Helios had men staking out the EGC townhouse waiting for Garrus to show. He never did.
- **Status:** Alive, drunk, somewhere in the city. Helios wants him. The party owes Helios a resolution.

### 🟡 6. Darric the Bouncer — Loose End
- Hellbrand Casino bouncer. Was paid off by Vaelis to give Marcell privacy on the night of the murder.
- Spooked after Marcell's death. Packed a bag and fled to the old Coaching Inn by the south gate.
- The party did not find him in Session 12. Helios is making enquiries about his whereabouts.
- **Status:** At the Coaching Inn. Has information about Vaelis. Hasn't been found yet.

### 🟢 7. The Feywild — Long Arc
- Mimbi is originally from the Feywild. The suppression of magic surges on the material plane is causing "bleaching" of Feywild inhabitants.
- The party briefly visited the Feywild (Session 9) but did not resolve anything there.
- **Status:** Background. Will resurface.

### 🟢 8. The Library — Ongoing Relationship
- The party are on good terms with **Kermin Traylow** (head of the Library, heavyset Dragonborn).
- The Library has a golden idol missing from a jungle island — unprotected, stolen by unknown parties. This may intersect with the Cornelius expedition.
- Books are going missing from the Library — magically tied volumes that should be impossible to remove permanently.
- **Status:** Background hooks, not yet activated.
