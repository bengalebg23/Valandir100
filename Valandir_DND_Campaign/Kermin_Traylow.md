[[Act 14 - The Gala]]

# Kermin Traylow
*Library Head — Gala Chaperone*

## Appearance
Heavyset silver-scaled dragonborn in deep blue ceremonial robes. Warm but careful. Moves through the room like a man managing twelve conversations simultaneously. His voice carries without effort — the natural projection of someone who has chaired difficult meetings for decades.

## Role at the Gala
Chaperone, speech-giver, and quiet guardian of the party's reputation. If the party looks lost he materialises at their elbow. If they cause a scene he materialises faster.

He has a private word specifically for [[Minbi Drugledamp]] — he knows she came to Valandir looking for answers about her people and believes the expedition may hold relevance beyond the city's crisis. He will not explain further tonight.

## Personality
- Warm but precise
- Manages people the way a good host manages a difficult dinner party — smoothly, invisibly
- Carries the weight of knowing Valandir is dying and telling almost no one
- Genuinely grateful to the party, in the restrained way of someone who was not certain they would survive

## Key Lines
> *"You came. Good. I wasn't entirely sure you would."*

> *"There are people here who are grateful for what you did. And people here who find gratitude... complicated. Stay close to me for the first half hour and let me do the navigating."*

> *"Hector de Léon arrived an hour ago. He's been asking about Hugo."*

Private word to Minbi:
> *"You came to Valandir looking for answers about your people. I know this. I believe the expedition Cornelius has described may hold answers relevant to more than just this city. I cannot explain further tonight. But I wanted you to know that I have not forgotten what you told me. And I do not think this is a coincidence."*

## What He Knows
- Valandir's crystals are running out — endpoint 5-15 years
- Commissioned Cornelius to pursue the legend of the Hearthstone
- Knows the Barkfield Reach is significant but not precisely why
- Has read the partial legend fragment — *"return them to the deep places"*
- Does not know the Feywild is the destination

## Connections
- [[Cornelius Thatch]] — commissioned him for the expedition
- [[Minbi Drugledamp]] — knows her Feywild connection
- [[Thrynn Rootscar]] — used him to track the druidic oral tradition
- [[Captain Crowe]] — considered him a necessary institution. Did not mourn him.
