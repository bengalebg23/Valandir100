# Valandir — Final Session Summary

## The End of the Crystal Thread

---

## The City Under Siege

The session opened with Valandir burning. Soren had moved — battle-mages teleported in across the city, the AG battered, the Library ransacked. He had come looking for the crystals and come close.

Hector de Léon was already at the Library steps when the party arrived, armour scored, holding a line. He had made good on his pledge from the gala without being asked.

Before the party left for the portal, he pulled Hugo aside.

> *"I'm proud of you. But you learned something I never did — you always gather everyone around a table before you act. I learned that from watching you. Don't lose it."*

---

## The Plan

No grand assault. No coordinated strike. The party made a pragmatic call: 100 crystals placed quickly would be enough to begin stabilising the Feywild. They teleported directly to bypass Soren's network entirely and began placing crystals across the convergence points — slowly at first, methodically, then with increasing urgency as the disturbance drew creatures toward them.

They carved through what got in the way and kept moving.

---

## The Otyugh

The Rift-Born Otyugh blocked the path to the portal. A creature that had pulled itself through a ley line rupture, enraged and enormous, standing between the party and the end of the campaign.

Mog pointed the Wand of Wonder at it.

A lucky roll. A very poor save.

The Otyugh became a daisy.

The party stepped over it and kept running.

---

## The Portal

The other monsters were too far back. The team pushed through to the portal site, placed the final crystal, and saw them coming — the Verdant Wrath descending from above, the rest of the horde racing across the ground toward them.

They did not stop to look through the portal at what was on the other side.

They ran.

---

## Mog's Last Stand (Brief)

Everyone made it through except Mog.

He had used the wand and somehow stunned himself in the process. He came around to find the Verdant Wrath dropping out of the sky directly toward him — a vast, ancient, furious thing, all vine and force and ancient rage.

He looked at it.

He thought Duck had been transformed by his own magic into his true form — some vast and terrible creature, finally revealed. He felt something close to awe. He may have worshipped, briefly.

The Wrath landed next to him. 28 damage. It did not particularly notice him.

Then he heard Duck. Behind him. Small. Definitely a duck. Definitely always a duck.

Mog ran.

The Wrath swung at him as he fled — opportunity attack, disadvantage, because Mog summoned a gust of butterflies from somewhere, which counts as a distraction. The attack missed.

Mog made it through the portal.

---

## The Fireball

Mimbi and Danerious were desperately working to dispel the portal as Mog came through.

Mog fired the Wand of Wonder one last time, back through the portal, at whatever was coming.

It fireballed the group.

Hugo went down. The first and only time in the entire campaign that Hugo de Léon fell — not to Soren Mourne, not to a rift-born monster, not in the defence of Valandir. To Mog. With a wand. By accident.

Mimbi and Danerious got the dispel off. The portal closed.

---

## Aftermath

The camera pulled back to a smouldering city. Valandir still standing. The AG battered. The Library scorched. Soren gone — whether dead, fled, or simply biding his time, unknown.

The team stayed the night at the Reach. Thrynn's land was already beginning to breathe again — the bleaching reversing at its edges, colour returning slowly to things that had been grey for too long.

The Crystal Thread was closed. The Feywild would heal.

Duck was, and remained, a duck.

---

## What Will Be Remembered

- The Otyugh became a daisy
- Mog briefly worshipped the Verdant Wrath
- Hugo de Léon survived every monster in Valandir and was felled by his own party's wand
- The Wrath, the Ghouls, the Crawlers, Soren's final scene — all of it — trapped on the other side of a portal that no longer exists
- Duck: innocent throughout

---

*Campaign complete. Sessions 1–16 (Final). The Valandir campaign — done.*
